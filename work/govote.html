Case study 02 2023 · Product Designer · CcHUB / GoVote

GOVOTE 2023 ELECTIONS

A voter-education platform with remarkably low traffic, confusing navigation and a look that didn't speak to young Nigerians. I redesigned it into a civic utility that sustained 2,000 sessions a day through election season.

Read on Medium ↗

The problem

Remarkably low traffic driven by three issues: insufficient information for first-time voters, navigation confusion, and an aesthetic that failed to engage youths 18+.

The solution

Restructured information architecture, a youth-centric design language, plus new content: Know Your Candidate, a polling-unit directory, voter education and election-day guides.

My role

Product Designer — research, IA, wireframes, style guide, components, hi-fi web & mobile. Working with a PM, dev team and DevOps.

The result

2,000 sessions/day, sustained — a pivotal role in voter engagement for Nigeria's 2023 General Elections.

01 Context & challenge

GoVote seeks to eliminate barriers and ensure every eligible Nigerian is equipped with the knowledge needed to participate in the electoral process. Ahead of the 2023 General Elections, the stakes couldn't have been higher — but the site wasn't earning its role.

Users experienced confusion due to unclear navigation, first-time voters couldn't find the information they needed, and the visual design failed to resonate with the youth demographic the platform most needed to reach.

02 My approach

Qualitative research with current visitors surfaced the information gaps and navigation obstacles. From there I recommended and designed:

  • Expanded content for first-time voters and election-day expectations
  • A voter education section and resources tab
  • "Know Your Candidate" — profiles to inform the ballot decision
  • A polling-unit directory so voters know exactly where to go
03 The redesign

High-fidelity designs across web and mobile, iterated with the team — clean hierarchy, a colour language young voters respond to, and every key task reachable in a couple of taps.

04 Result

The redesign attained commendable success — the website consistently garnered 2,000 sessions per day, with positive feedback underscoring its impact. The platform played a pivotal role in the 2023 elections in Nigeria, contributing significantly to voter engagement and information dissemination — recognised publicly by the Managing Partner at CcHUB.

RAPID RESPONSE

A design sprint with The Guardian and FT Strategies — from How-Might-We framing and storyboarding to a user-tested mobile prototype, at newsroom speed.

View on Behance ↗

The brief

Compress months of product decisions into one focused sprint — validate a new mobile experience for reaching readers faster, before committing build resources.

The method

The classic sprint arc: understand → HMW → sketch → decide → prototype → test, run collaboratively on MURAL and Figma.

My role

Designer & sprint facilitator — user research, HMW framing, storyboarding, wireframes, UI and the tested prototype.

The outcome

A user-tested prototype in 5 days — evidence in hand before a single line of production code.

03 How I think

"I take a proactive UX approach — aligning with stakeholders on the reasoning behind our actions, prioritising the highest-value work, and letting empirical research drive every decision."

01

Align

Agree the "why" with stakeholders before pixels move. Prioritise by highest value for users, customers or the business.

Stakeholder workshops · Success metrics
02

Research

Interviews, surveys and journey maps — one per stakeholder, so each group's pains and emotions stay visible.

Interviews · Surveys · Personas · CJMs
03

Ideate

Diverge fast with 10-for-10 sketching and How-Might-We framing, then converge on what evidence supports.

10-for-10 · HMW · Crazy 8s
04

Prototype

User flows to wireframes to a hi-fi, componentised prototype — with a style guide so the system outlives the file.

Flows · Wireframes · Design system · Figma
05

Test & iterate

Usability tests with real users, refine, retest. Excellence over perfection — ship the thing that works.

Usability testing · Iteration
06

Ship & measure

Launch, watch the data, and feed what we learn back into the next cycle. Design is never "done" ✧˖°

Analytics · Retros · Next cycle
